Welcome to Prachinburi

Prachinburi is a province in the eastern region of Thailand. It is a city with a long history. Ancient remains have been found in many areas of the province. In addition, there are many natural tourist attractions. There are 3 national parks in the World Heritage Area. It is also the province with the most forest area in the eastern region.
